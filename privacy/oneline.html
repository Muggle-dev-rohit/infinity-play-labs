3. Information We Collect

Personal Information:

OneLine does not require account creation and does not collect personal information such as name, email, or phone number.

Journal Entries:

Your journal entries are stored locally on your device and are never transmitted to our servers.

Usage Data (via Firebase):

We use Google Firebase to collect anonymized data such as:

  • Device type and OS version
  • App usage behavior
  • Crash reports and performance data

4. No Ads Policy

OneLine is completely ad-free.

We do not use advertising services and do not collect any data for advertising purposes.

5. Permissions

The app may use internet access for Firebase analytics.

We do not access:

  • Camera
  • Microphone
  • Contacts
  • Photos or files

6. Data Security

Your personal journal data remains on your device.

We do not store or share your personal content.

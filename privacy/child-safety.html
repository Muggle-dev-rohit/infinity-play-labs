Child Safety Standards

Last Updated: March 2026

1. Our Commitment

Oneline is committed to providing a safe and respectful environment for all users. We strictly prohibit any form of child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE) content.

2. Zero Tolerance Policy

We enforce a zero-tolerance policy against any content that involves or promotes harm to minors. Any violation will result in immediate removal of content and appropriate action.

3. Content Monitoring

  • We actively monitor user-generated content
  • We take action against harmful or inappropriate posts
  • We continuously improve safety mechanisms

4. Reporting Mechanism

Users can report harmful or inappropriate content. We review all reports seriously and take necessary action to maintain a safe environment.

5. Compliance with Laws

We comply with all applicable child safety laws and cooperate with law enforcement authorities when required.

6. Data Safety

We do not support, promote, or allow any illegal activities involving minors. User safety remains our highest priority.
